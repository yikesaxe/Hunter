Ideally Located

This furnished apartment is situated in Williamsburg, known as Brooklyns creative outlet. Residents vary from artists and young professionals working in the media or entertainment industries to families. Williamsburg Bridge is great for long strolls, running or biking. Also the M and J trains make it easy to get to other downtown neighborhoods.
